Tai Ping Hou Kui review

May 22, 2020
1 min read

Make sure to get yourself a cuppa before reading on!

tea: Tai Ping Hou Kui
type: loose leaf green tea
tea house: Tao Tea Leaf

This tea is EXTREMELY subtle, but very clear in its taste! Both the scent and taste are much less apparent, but its freshness and floral notes are easily distinguishable. It’s sweet, and has almost a ‘warming-soul’ taste to it, much like the feel you get from chai.

Overall rating: 8/10, taste is a little too subtle lol
