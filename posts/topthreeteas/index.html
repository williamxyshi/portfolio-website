Top Three Teas

May 28, 2020
2 min read

What are y’all sipping? I’m drinking some Hojicha right now. Make sure to brew yourself a cuppa before reading!

I thought I would preface this list by saying that these three teas are in no particular order, and that they’re my three favourite teas that I’ve tried so far. Additionally, I will not be factoring in the tea house, just favourite teas in general.

Dragon Pearls

green tea, scented

This tea is a no brainer, the look and the taste are all A+. The scent of jasmine compliments, but doesn’t overpower the natural taste of green tea. The hand rolled tea unfurls during its brewing process, turning from small pearls into full tea leaves.
I currently own three dragon pearls, i have 100g from Camellia Sinensis, Tao Tea Leaf and DavidsTea.

Genmaicha

green tea, blend(?)

This tea is an absolute classic. The smell and taste of popped rice IS overpowering, but somehow the sencha flavour still shines through. It’s a very ‘cozy’ tea, tastes almost like popcorn, and looks super interesting.
I currently own two genmaichas, 100g from Camellia Sinensis and 200g from ito en.

Masala Chai

black tea, blend

The first two in this list were no brainers, but picking a third was hard. Masala chai clutched the list because of it’s spicy, fragrant personality.
I currently own 100g of Vahdam Tea’s Masala chai, a tea house that specialized in indian teas.



Have a great day!
