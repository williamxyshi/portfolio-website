Tetley's Earl Grey review

May 23, 2020
1 min read

Can’t go wrong with some earl grey loaded with milk and sugar!

tea: Earl Grey
type: bagged black tea, scented
tea house: Tetley

80 tea bags for 6 bucks?! Hard to say no to that value. It’s true that the flavor doesn’t quite compare to other earl greys, but with sugar and milk added in, it tastes awesome!

Overall rating: 6.5/10, classic liquor but isn’t special
