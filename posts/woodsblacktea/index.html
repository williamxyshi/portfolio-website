Woods Family Blend Review

Jul 15, 2020
1 min read

Hey everyone! Long time no see, sorry for not posting in a while, I’ve been busy at work and working hard on some other projects( Plant Doctor (NOT mobile friendly), game jams and a third incomplete/unreleased project) but rest assured, I’ve been sippin!

tea: Woods Family Blend
type: loose leaf black tea, blend
tea house: Woods Family

Being a black tea with bergamot, rose petas and lavender, it surpasses many “professional” tea house blends that I’ve seen to date. It looks great, the dried flower petals look super cool, and it smells great too.

I really enjoyed drinking this tea, I would say the taste matches well to how it smells, and it lingers around for a while. The bergamot works well with the rose and lavender, and I like that it’s more of a supporting flavour. It doesn’t hold up well past the first steep, just quickly losing flavour on successive steeps.

Overall rating: 7.5/10, not an everyday tea (no black tea is for me) but a great tea to have around
