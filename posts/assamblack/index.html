Assam Black Review

May 30, 2020
1 min read

I was looking for a high caffeine tea when I bought this a few months ago, so I chose an assam for its full-bodied malty flavour.

tea: Assam Black
type: loose leaf black tea
tea house: Distinctly Tea

The grade on this tea is TGFOP, and the price was fair. The tea smells exactly like I hoped, a strong black tea.

The leaves brew into a dark orange brew, and smells a bit more woody than the leaves did. Tastes better with some milk (and sugar), which is probably just my personal preference.

Overall rating: 5/10, got what I was looking for
