teashop.tv

Teashop.tv is an NFT marketplace for content creators to sell, and fans to buy short video clips.
NFT technology allows fans to collect and personally own the moments they watch from their favorite content creators. Every moment is minted to the Ethereum blockchain with a unique serial number (NFT), which means that not only are they limited edition, their ownership is also secure. From history making esports moments to epic gaming plays, hilarious streamer clips and everything inbetween,
our marketplace allows content creators to sell unique moments, and fans to buy memories.
