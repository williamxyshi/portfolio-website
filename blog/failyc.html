failing the yc interview

back to blog | back to home
we were extremely excited when we recieved our invitation to interview for YC W23 on sept 28th. They wanted to meet the following day so we quickly scrambled to prepare however we could. After talking with founders at startups that went through yc, they all had similar advice for the interview - be concise since the interview is only 10 minutes, focus on selling yourself since yc invests in founders etc.
