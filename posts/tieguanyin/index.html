Tie Guan Yin - Top Grade review

May 24, 2020
1 min read

I’m a big fan of tie guan yin (iron goddess of mercy), so this top grade version was super exciting to try out!

tea: Tie Guan Yin
type: loose leaf oolong
tea house: Tao Tea Leaf

Being a regular drinker of Tao Tea Leaf’s premium grade Tie Guan Yin, it’s hard to notice a difference between the top grade and the regular premium grade in terms of taste. The taste is marginally more earthy, and the scent a bit more floral.

While the taste remains pretty much the same, the leaves hold up much better over multiple steeps. Past the third resteep, the top grade tea becomes much sweeter, while the premium grade tea starts fading in flavour.

Overall rating: 9/10, I’m a sucker for good iron goddess tea, and this pretty much lives up to the hype
