Golden Monkey Black Review

Jun 03, 2020
1 min read

Get yourself a cuppa before reading this review :) One of the most famous black teas, the Golden Monkey tea is supposed to taste rich, with many complex flavours. Also the leaves resemble monkey paws? not sure about that one..

tea: Golden Monkey Black Tea
type: loose leaf black, scented
tea house: Tao Tea Leaf

Leaves dont have a particular smell, but like a blend of scents. The leaves do not look like monkey paws, just they have golden tips which looks pretty cool i guess. Each leaf is extremely thin, and very fragile - I broke like half the leaves i used today just opening the pack.

The tea has hints of apples and nuts which I can pick up and spice notes in the aftertaste. Tastes better on the second and third steeps, but begins to fall off after that.

Overall rating: 7/10, great black tea but i’m not a fan of black teas - yet
