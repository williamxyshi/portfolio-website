Madame La Marquise Review

May 31, 2020
1 min read

I grabbed this tea because it’s blended with apricot and it’s from one of my favourite tea houses, Camellia Sinensis in Montreal.

tea: Madame la Marquise
type: loose leaf oolong tea, blend
tea house: Camellia Sinensis

Doesn’t smell much like apricot, but instead very fruity and creamy. The tea looks really cool too, and the leaves fully open up when brewing.

The taste is extremely creamy, and fruity as well. The apricots, despite their size, don’t actually taste as strong as it might seem - only hints of the apricot can be picked up. The second and third brews start losing the creamy taste rapidly, and reveals the taste of the base oolong.

Overall rating: 6/10, didn’t get what I expected, but it was pretty good I guess
