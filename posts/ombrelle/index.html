Ombrelle De Papier Review

Jun 01, 2020
1 min read

Ingredients : white tea, green tea, pineapple, lemongrass, raspberry leaves, morello cherry, sunflower petals, lemon myrtle, rhubarb, strawberry, pear.
I picked up this tea for the absolutely bonkers ingredients, and also low price! Yes, I’m a camellia sinensis fanboy.

tea: Ombrelle de Papier
type: loose leaf white tea, blend
tea house: Camellia Sinensis

This blended tea looks out of this world. Every ingredient listed can be seperately identified - especially the giant strawberry slices! The tea smells exactly like it should, a fresh fruity melody with an overpowering smell of strawberry. It was almost too fruity, I was nervous it would be too sweet with all those fruits.

The liquor itself smells like the leaves did, but it actually does not taste sweet at all! The fruity flavour does not overpower the taste of white tea, but rather they blend very nicely. It’s not sweet at all, and VERY refreshing to sip. Fruity flavours fade after the first brew.

Overall rating: 7.5/10, looks cool, tastes cool
